State Cultures of India

Rajasthani

Known for royal palaces, Ghoomar dance, and folk traditions.

Gujarati

Famous for Garba, Dandiya, and traditional vegetarian food.

Bengali

Durga Puja, Rabindra Sangeet, and fish-based cuisine shine here.

Marathi

Ganeshotsav, Lavani dance and the Maratha pride.

Assamese

Celebrated for Bihu festival, Muga silk, and tea estates.

Kashmiri

Known for Pashmina, Kahwa tea, and poetic traditions.

Tamil

Bharatanatyam, Pongal, temples and Sangam literature.

Bihari / UP

Rich traditions from Ramayana, Chhath, and folk music.
